Welcome to Clover Traffic Connections!

Here at Bloomington Traffic Connections we're committed to giving our users a smooth introduction to the world of data interpretation. Our site helps users to understand and visualize the copious amount of information that comes with living in a city such as Bloomington, IN. The data present on our website will help in planning your daily route and avoiding high risk areas. For one reason or another traffic accidents tend to happen in specific problem areas and we’re doing our part to help prepare our users when driving in these locations and reduce the amount of collisions in our fine city.

Information found on our site includes data of past collisions and outlines the types of vehicles involved as well as the severity of the incident. It doesn’t stop there though, we also possess data about widely used biking routes for those who would prefer to bike to their locations or just get some exercise. No matter the type of citizen you are, our site will give you something useful and improve your daily life in Bloomington.

Important Safety Reminders

  • Click it or ticket. Don't forget to wear your seatbelt!
  • Faster isn’t always better. Driving safely is more important than getting to your destination as quickly as possible.
  • Everyone needs their space. Give vehicles a very comfortable following distance.
  • Watch for pedestrians. We live in a big city with lots of people and the road is also shared with cyclists be sure they have space as well!
